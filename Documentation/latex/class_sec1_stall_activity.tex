\doxysubsection{Detailed Description}
Section 1 stall training activity controller. 

Manages a progressive pitch exercise where users practice increasing angle of attack through six stages (3°, 7°, 10°, 14°, 16°, 18°) while observing aerodynamic effects. Enables real-\/time visualization of force arrows, airflow particles, and center of pressure to demonstrate the relationship between pitch angle and aerodynamic forces leading to stall. Implements the \doxylink{interface_i_activity_controller}{IActivity\+Controller} interface for integration with the \doxylink{class_module_activity_scheduler}{Module\+Activity\+Scheduler} system.

Training sequence\+:
\begin{DoxyEnumerate}
\item Pitch -\/ Progressive pitch targets with aerodynamic visualization
\item Complete -\/ Activity finished, notifies scheduler
\end{DoxyEnumerate}

Key features\+:
\begin{DoxyItemize}
\item Real-\/time force arrow visualization (lift, weight, thrust, drag)
\item Procedural airflow particle system
\item Dynamic center of pressure indicator
\item Progressive angle of attack demonstration leading to stall
\end{DoxyItemize}

void Sec1\+Stall\+Activity.\+Handle\+Pitching (\begin{DoxyParamCaption}{}{}\end{DoxyParamCaption})}



Handle progressive pitch training step. 

Captures controller origin on first frame, reads current pitch angle, and checks if user has achieved the current target angle. When target is reached, locks input, plays confirmation timeline, and advances to next pitch target or completion. The progression through six pitch angles (3, 7, 10, 14, 16, 18) demonstrates increasing aerodynamic forces and changing center of pressure as the aircraft approaches stall conditions. Allows controller or debug mouse input when target not yet achieved.

Aerodynamic visualizations update in real-\/time\+:
\begin{DoxyItemize}
\item Force arrows show changing lift, drag, thrust, and weight magnitudes
\item Particle system shows airflow separation at higher angles
\item Center of pressure indicator moves along chord as angle increases 
\end{DoxyItemize}
