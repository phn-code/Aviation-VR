Determines and updates behaviour of particles. 

Creates a local copy of the assigned Particle System\textquotesingle{}s particle buffer and modifies the tranform of particles. Particles follow the calculated radius and the logic is functional during level, ascending and descending flight.

Formula for calculating turning radius   \[R = \frac{V^2}{g \times \tan(\theta)}
\] where\+:
\begin{DoxyItemize}
\item $R$ = Radius (m)
\item $V$ = Velocity (m/s)
\item $g$ = Gravity (m/s²)
\item $\theta$ = Bank angle (degrees)
\end{DoxyItemize}

Particles transforms are modified and buffer is returned to the Particle system.
